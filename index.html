Welcome to Robocode-BKI-Hunter Project

The Hunter robot main objective is to stalk the closest opponent at every turn. As a stalker, the robot will try to maintain its distance from the enemy by circling around. At every opportunity, it will try predictive firing in hopes of hitting the enemy.
